# Research

## Research Interests

- **Photonic Chips** — Design, simulation, and fabrication of integrated photonic circuits for communication and sensing applications.
- **RF Front-End** — Radio frequency front-end modules, including antenna interfaces, filters, and amplifiers for wireless systems.
- **3D Packaging** — Advanced three-dimensional heterogeneous integration and packaging technologies for electronic and optoelectronic systems.
- **Laser Micro/Nano Fabrication** — Precision laser-based manufacturing techniques for micro- and nano-scale structures and devices.

## Current Projects

- Terahertz device fabrication and theoretical modeling
- Optical waveguide design and characterization
- Advanced packaging for integrated photonic-electronic systems

## Research Highlights

My research bridges the gap between fundamental device physics and practical system integration, with a focus on next-generation optoelectronic and RF technologies.
