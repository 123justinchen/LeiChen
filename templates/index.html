About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Shandong University, with a research focus on optical and terahertz (THz) device fabrication and theoretical modeling. My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Optoelectronic Information from the University of Jinan (2017–2021), followed by a Master's degree in Electronic Information from Beijing University of Technology (2021–2024). Between 2024 and 2025, I worked at two research institutes, where I was involved in engineering projects related to CPT atomic magnetometers and single-photon Doppler wind lidar systems.
